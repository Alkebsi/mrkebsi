Things I Love To Write About

Since I was a small child, I was feeling that something works inside my heart once I see the small, children comic books. Most of the children in my age were only love to look at the images and that is it, but I was always struggling to understand the words and the main theme of what I am looking at. This stimulated my hobby and passion in reading. After elementary school, I started my first Arabic blog. And now, I feel that English blogs are the way I may express my passion to the world. Anyhow, I usually write about...


  • Coding or Programming,
  • Marketing,
  • English Language,
  • Personal Stories, or
  • Other Interesting Topics, e.g. Music, Photography, Design...etc



Why Am I Writing?

I believe that the main reason has been mentioned above, but I still have some others. First, I want a way to practice typing on my computer when I don't have a project to work on. Then I believe that doing something is better than nothing, so I love getting anything out of my heart since this will keep teaching me new things. Lastly, I usually feel that I must pay back to the community whether financially or knowledgeably. So, I started teaching English locally and write globally. This really treats the soul, the heart and the brain once you understand that what you are doing is helping someone somewhere, isn't it?
