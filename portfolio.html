SiZillion - A Platform for Web Responsiveness Testing

In 2019, I was very enthusiastic about being a Websites Developer/Designer, but one of the biggest challenges in my life started there. I didn't had a laptop at that time. I managed, after great difficulties, to code using my mobile phone. At a time, I remembered that I had a small document that was able to test the width and height of my other documents.


I searched for it and developed it, actually still developing it, to something more sophisticated and neatly designed. This thing made me keep using my phone in some web apps even after a long professional career. After all, a mobile is much more flexible than PC, but for some touches and nothing more.


Being able to test my new document in different devices made even better for me to understand how will my site look like in 32 inches monitor while I have a 12 inches laptop! Having more than 1 functionality, I was able to exploit things that I have never thought of.


Techniques Used in This Project

  • HTML, CSS and Javascript
  • Gradients
  • Dark Theme

I also think of making it open source and help others how had my problem in the old days. I don't know what can I say more, but at least, this is another thing I want to help the community with. Even it's just a simple testing tool, it may help someone somewhere!
